How to Use BSCS1B TASK APP
Welcome to BSCS1B TaskHub

The top section is the first thing you see. It automatically shows your most urgent tasks â€” things that are overdue, due today, or due within 3 days. Pinned notes from admins also appear here.

Use the filter buttons on the left to quickly find what you need:

Done Tasks you have personally marked as completed on your device.
Reading a Task Card
Each task card shows the task name, its category (e.g. Quiz, Exam, Project), the due date and time, and a status chip (Soon, Today, Overdue, Done).

The card preview only shows a short description. Tap View to expand the card and read the full description, see attached images, and access action buttons.

Once expanded, tap Close to collapse it back.
